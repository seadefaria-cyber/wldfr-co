FOUNDERS AGREEMENT, WLDFR

Date: ___________________________

This Founders Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by the undersigned individuals ("Founders") for the purpose of establishing the terms of their partnership in building the business known as WLDFR (Wildfire), a digital marketing agency. This is a pre-formation agreement intended to govern the relationship between the Founders until a formal LLC operating agreement is executed. This agreement is intended to be legally binding upon execution by all parties.

1. Founders and Equity

  • Amy 최아리, 30%
  • Lukas Levine, 40%
  • Sean DeFaria, 30%

No founder's equity may be diluted without that founder's written consent.

2. Roles and Responsibilities

  • Amy 최아리: Strategy, management, supervision, pitching, and client presentations. Amy brings over 10 years of agency experience.
  • Lukas Levine: Client acquisition and relationship management. Lukas is primarily responsible for bringing in and maintaining client relationships.
  • Sean DeFaria: Digital marketing operations, AI technology and automation, team training, and technical infrastructure. Sean introduced the AI-driven digital marketing workflow that serves as the foundation of WLDFR's operations.

3. Decision Making and Governance

Day-to-day operations decisions may be made by any founder within their defined role. The following require unanimous consent of all three founders: new hires, any single expense over $5,000, new client contracts over $25,000, changes to equity, adding new founders, and dissolution of the company.

No two founders may combine their equity to force out, dilute, or overrule the third founder on matters of removal, equity changes, or dissolution. Any decision to remove a founder, change equity splits, or dissolve the company requires unanimous consent.

4. LLC Formation

The founders agree to form an LLC within 90 days of signing the first paying client, or within 6 months of this agreement, whichever comes first. The state of formation will be decided by unanimous agreement.

5. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of New York.

6. Termination

This agreement terminates and is superseded upon execution of a formal LLC operating agreement by all founders.

This agreement is intended to be legally binding upon execution by all parties.
