FOUNDERS AGREEMENT, WLDFR

Date: ___________________________

This is a simple founders agreement between the three of us as we start building WLDFR (Wildfire), a digital marketing agency. This is not an operating agreement. This just puts on paper that we're doing this together, who owns what, and the basics of how we work. Everything else, profit splits, vesting, IP, day-to-day policies, all of that gets handled properly in the LLC operating agreement once we form the entity. This is just the handshake on paper.

1. Founders and Equity

  • Amy 최아리, 30%
  • Lukas Levine, 40%
  • Sean DeFaria, 30%

No founder's equity may be diluted without that founder's written consent.

2. Roles and Responsibilities

  • Amy 최아리: Strategy, management, supervision, client acquisition, pitching, and client presentations. Amy brings over 10 years of agency experience.
  • Lukas Levine: Client acquisition and relationship management. Lukas is primarily responsible for bringing in and maintaining client relationships.
  • Sean DeFaria: AI technology and infrastructure, editorial team management and hiring, and digital marketing operations and execution.

3. Decision Making and Governance

All decisions affecting the company's finances, equity, partnerships, or direction require unanimous consent of all three founders. No two founders may combine their equity to force out, dilute, or overrule the third founder on any matter.

If a founder stops contributing to WLDFR for 30 consecutive days without written agreement from the other founders, that founder forfeits their equity, which will be redistributed proportionally to the remaining founders.

4. Outside Work

No founder is exclusively bound to WLDFR. Each founder may take on outside work, clients, and projects as long as that work does not directly compete with an active WLDFR engagement.

5. LLC Formation

The founders agree to form an LLC within 90 days of signing the first paying client, or within 6 months of this agreement, whichever comes first. The state of formation will be decided by unanimous agreement.

6. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of New York.

7. Termination

This agreement terminates and is superseded upon execution of a formal LLC operating agreement by all founders.

This agreement is intended to be legally binding upon execution by all parties.
